How We Play

Skins

One skin per hole to the player with the lowest net score. Ties carry forward, compounding until won outright.

Presses

A player behind on round skins may press at the start of a hole. Doubles (or increments) per-hole skin value from the pressed hole onward — never on earlier holes, and never greenies (those stay fixed). Max 3; third needs back nine. Called a press on the wrong hole? Use its chip to move or cancel it — skins recalculate instantly.

Birdies & Eagles

Win a hole and, on top of its value (including presses), earn a bonus based on your gross score: a true birdie adds a bonus equal to the hole's pressed value; a true eagle adds twice it; a true double eagle adds three times it. (So a 2×-pressed hole won with a birdie pays 2 + 2 = 4; won with an eagle pays 2 + 4 = 6.) A gross par that nets to a birdie via handicap strokes adds a single flat bonus skin instead. The bonus applies only to that hole's skin, not to carried-in skins, and can be switched off in the Rules settings.

Carryovers

Each carried skin is locked at the value of the hole it was won on. A later press lifts only the holes from the press onward — it never re-values skins carried from before it.

Greenies

Closest to the pin in regulation on par-3s earns a greenie — a fixed skin value that presses and birdie/eagle bonuses never change.

Reverse Greenies

Three-putt your greenie? Lose it; every other player earns a reverse greenie, also a fixed skin value.

Nine & Round Bonuses

Lowest net F9, B9, and total round each pay a bonus. Ties split evenly.

Daily Handicaps

Each round lets you override each player's handicap index. GHIN updates daily — use today's number.

Stroke Allocation

Each player's course handicap is calculated from their index, tees, and the allowance. Then the lowest handicap in the group plays at scratch, and others receive strokes equal to the difference — applied on the hardest holes by stroke index. This is the USGA standard for net play between mixed handicaps.

The Monkey Butt

Annual loser (fewest skins, calendar year) bags the Monkey Butt head cover for the year. Winner takes glory.

How to Use

  1. Start a round. Go to Today's Round, pick the trip, course, tees and who's playing (handicaps pre-fill), then Start Round.
  2. Enter scores. Tap a hole to open it, then use the − / + buttons to set each gross score — no keyboard needed. Net and strokes fill in automatically.
  3. Skins. Lowest net score wins the hole. Ties carry to the next hole. On a win you also earn a bonus based on your gross score: true birdie +1× the hole's pressed value, true eagle +2×, true double eagle +3×; a gross par that nets to a birdie adds +1 flat skin (toggle in Rules).
  4. Press. Tap the red (P) on a hole to raise the stakes from that hole onward. Pick who's pressing — the current leader can't press.
  5. Greenies. On par 3s, mark closest-to-the-pin — worth a fixed skin (presses and birdies/eagles don't change greenies). Three-putt it and everyone else gets a reverse greenie.
  6. Extra holes. If skins are still carrying after 18, add a Playoff Hole (choose which hole it plays), or carry the pot into the next round when you finalize.
  7. Finalize. Finalize Round at the bottom logs it to the ledger. Need to fix it later? Edit it from the Rounds tab.
  8. Trips & Hall. Rounds group into Trips. Finalize a trip to crown its Champion and curse its Monkey Butt in the Hall of Fame & Shame.
  9. Everyone stays in sync. Anyone who opens this link shares the same live game. The header shows ● Synced and the last completed hole.
